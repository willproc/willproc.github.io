Non-Fiction Anim Project

This was a short film I made for a university project, and I used Substance Designer to create the inked look. I rendered out passes and played around with the depth info in Substance Designer to build up those layered ink effects. I also animated some of the parameters in the Substance Graph using $TIME to add a bit of weird, wobbly distortion to the visuals. Then, I ran all the different passes through Substance Player as sequences, which gave me a bunch of unique elements to stack on top of each other for the final look. It was a fun way to experiment.
